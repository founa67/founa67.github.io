Samba Bar & Restaurant: 15-Year Performance Dashboard
Power BI Business Intelligence Data Visualization Performance Analytics

This project involved designing and developing an end-to-end Power BI business intelligence solution for Samba Bar & Restaurant, a Kenya-based establishment.

The interactive dashboard consolidates and analyzes 15 years of operational data (2009–2024), transforming raw, disparate data into a clear, strategic asset for management and executives.

The solution provides a 360-degree view of the business, enabling data-driven decision-making for sales strategy, menu optimization, staff performance, and geographic expansion.

BREMEN BIKE GmbH – Sales Dashboard Project
Excel Data Transformation Customer Analytics Dashboard

This project demonstrates the end-to-end process of data cleaning, transformation, and dashboard development using Microsoft Excel.

The dataset represents customer and sales information for BREMEN BIKE GmbH, a bicycle retailer. The goal was to:

  • Prepare and clean raw sales data for accuracy and consistency.
  • Engineer new features (age groups, commute distance, income groups).
  • Build an interactive Excel dashboard for management to analyze customer demographics and purchasing behavior.
